### Discipline (Anushasan)

Discipline ko arth ho niyam ma rahi, aafno kartabya pura garne banai. Je pani kaam kramma garnu parcha, tyo niyamit tarikale garnu discipline ho. Discipline le mancheko jeevan ma sudhar lyauchha ra safal banaunchha. 

Prakriti ko niyam haru pani discipline ko udaharan ho. Din ra raat ko gatibidhi, surya ko udai ra astai, tatha mausam ko parivartan sabai discipline ma baseko ho. Yedi prakriti le niyam ko palana nagareko bhaye, sabai kura bigrathyo. Tyasaile, discipline le vyakti, samaj ra desh ko unnati ko aadhar bancha.

Discipline hamile sabai thau ma dekhnu sakincha, jastai school, ghar, karyalaya ra samaj ma. School ma discipline le hamro padhai ramro banauchha. Ghar ma discipline le parivar ma shanti ra sukh lyauchha. Karyalaya ma discipline le kaamko gunastariya banauchha. Samaj ma discipline le shanti ra ekata banauchha.

Discipline ko abhav le afnai jindagima asafalta ra samaj ma astavyasta lyauchha. Niyam palana nagareko le jhagada, golmal ra anya samasya uthanchha. Tyasaile, hami sabai le discipline ko mahatwa bujhera aafno jeevan ma apnau parcha. Discipline ek manav ko pehchaan ho ra yasto gun ho, jun le vyakti lai safal ra sammanit banauchha.

Tyesaile, hami sabai le aafno jeevan ma discipline lai aadar garna siknu parcha ra yo gun sadhai jivit rakhnuparcha. Discipline bina jeevan aadhura ra asafal huncha, tyasaile yo hamro jeevan ko mul mantra hunuparcha.
